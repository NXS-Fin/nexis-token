# NEXIS (NXS) — Whitepaper v2.0

## Abstract

NEXIS (NXS) is a deflationary ERC-20 token deployed on Ethereum Mainnet 
on July 1, 2026. The protocol implements automatic burn, auto-liquidity 
and staking mechanics with a 48-hour timelock on all administrative changes.

## 1. Introduction

NEXIS is built on Ethereum as a sovereign liquidity infrastructure token. 
With a fixed maximum supply of 444,444,444 NXS and continuous burn 
mechanism, NXS becomes increasingly scarce with every transaction.

### 2.1 Burn Mechanism

Every transfer automatically burns 0.5% of the transferred amount.
Total supply decreases with every transaction.

**Example:**
- Transfer: 1,000 NXS
- Burned: 5 NXS (0.5%)
- Liquidity: 10 NXS (1%)
- Received: 985 NXS

### 2.2 Auto-Liquidity

1% of every transaction is automatically sent to the 
designated liquidity wallet to support market stability.

### 2.3 Staking Rewards

Token holders can stake NXS to earn 10% APY rewards
distributed from the dedicated reward pool.

### 2.4 Timelock Security

All administrative changes are subject to a mandatory 
48-hour timelock, preventing sudden modifications.
