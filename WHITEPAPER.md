# NEXIS (NXS) — Whitepaper v1.0

## Abstract

NEXIS (NXS) is a deflationary ERC-20 token deployed on Ethereum Mainnet.
The protocol implements an automatic burn mechanism of 0.5% on every 
transaction, reducing total supply over time.

---

## 1. Introduction

The NEXIS token is designed as a study in deflationary tokenomics on 
Ethereum. With a fixed maximum supply and a continuous burn mechanism, 
NXS becomes increasingly scarce with every transaction.

### 2.1 Burn Mechanism

Every transfer automatically burns 0.5% of the transferred amount.
This means the total supply decreases with every transaction,
creating deflationary pressure over time.

**Example:**
- Transfer: 1,000 NXS
- Burned: 5 NXS
- Received: 995 NXS
